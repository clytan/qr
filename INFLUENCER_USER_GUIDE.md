## â“ FAQ

**Q: How do I become an influencer on Zokli?**
A: Simply log in and access the Influencer Program from the footer menu. No special registration required!

**Q: Can I accept multiple collaborations?**
A: Yes! Accept as many as you can realistically deliver on time.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Payment terms are decided with the brand directly. Usually after deliverables are submitted and approved.

**Q: What if I can't deliver?**
A: Contact the brand immediately. Professional communication is key.

**Q: Can I negotiate terms?**
A: Initial terms are set by the brand. You can discuss modifications via email after accepting.

**Q: How are payments processed?**
A: Payments are handled directly between you and the brand. Details will be shared via email.

**Q: Can I turn down a collab after accepting?**
A: Only in exceptional circumstances. Accepting is a commitment. Always read requirements before accepting.

**Q: What happens to completed collaborations?**
A: They move to your "My Collaborations" history for reference.
